Mriya Site Overview

Mriya site, this site is about the "dream" plane, the dream is the largest plane created by mankind, it was created in Ukraine by the Kyiv Antonov factory.

This site is made about the dream plane, this sat describes the largest aircraft in the world, the An-225 "Mriya" (NATO code name - "Cossack" in English Cossack) - a super-heavy transport aircraft, created in 1988 in the USSR by the Antonov Design Bureau of Kyiv. By 2022, the An-225 was the largest and most powerful aircraft in the world. The chief designer is Viktor Tolmachov. The aircraft was created for the needs of the Soviet space program: to transport cargo, the Buran spacecraft and components of the Energia missile system.
